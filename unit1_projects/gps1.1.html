Wild Card

Driver


As it relates to peer-programming, the Driver will handle the typing, worrying about the details to ensure the code is entered properly, is syntactically correct, and indented appropriately to be user-friendly.
Tips on being a great driver:

  • Be attentive to your pair.
  • "The driver presses the gas, but the navigator says when to turn."
  • "The driver gives input, but should be focused on the area of code instructed by the navigator."

Navigator


The Navigator will be telling the Driver where to go. It is the Navigator that dictates what code will be written, relying on the Driver to get him there.
Tips on being a great navigator:

  • Communicate your plan generally before diving into specifics.
  • Be willing to accept input should your pair feel the need to interject.
  • Know when to let your pair drive.
