Margins, Borders, and Padding - A Demystification

If you're like me, at one point or another you have been confused about the difference between margins, borders, and padding. But sometimes all you need are visual aids to put things in perspective.

Padding

Let's start with the padding. The padding is a sort of insulation between the content and the border.

Margins

Margins, as they are in word processors are the space from the border to the adjacent edge. Often this is the edge of the web page, but not necessarily always.

Borders

The border is the definite boundary around the content, nested between the padding and the margin. It is often the most concrete and recognizable of the three.
